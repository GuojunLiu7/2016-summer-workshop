ISCB DC Area Regional Student Group - Summer 2016 Workshop

June 15, 2016

Welcome to the homepage for the ISCB Washington DC metropolitan area regional student group (RSG 2016 summer workshop!

One June 15, 2016, we will be hosting a free one-day workshop series with hand-on/open laptop workshops focused on various topics in bioinformatics, computational biology and genomics. The workshop is student driven, with primarily graduate students and post-docs leading the various discussions, but open to anyone interested - students, post-docs, faculty, staff scientists, etc.

There will be several workshop sessions throughout the day, and at each session, attendees can choose from one of two topics of interest to them. For a complete list of workshop topics, see the "schedule" section below.
